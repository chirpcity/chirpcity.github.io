About ChirpCity

A hub for resources to help birds in the Chicago area.

Founded at Civic Hack Day 2015 at the Adler Planetarium, our goal is to provide resources that will help ordinary Chicagoans to do their part to help conserve birds in Chicago. This page was created by a group of hackers, educators, and nature enthusiasts. Using information from Lincoln Park Zoo’s Urban Wildlife Institute and other birding authorities, we identified two main issues facing Chicago’s birds: 1) birds eating human food and 2) birds colliding with windows during migration season. Read on to learn more about how birds live in Chicago and how you can help protect them.
