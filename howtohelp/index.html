How to help

Feeding birds

As fun as it may be to feed birds, it is very dangerous to the birds health. Bread is not nutritious for birds, and they learn to be dependent on humans for food. If you want to see birds, go to a park near you, and you can harmlessly observe birds.

Windows

Sure, buildings with lots of windows are nice to look at. However, when birds are migrating, they may not realize that there is a window in their way. Bright lights in buildings can also be confusing to them. This is a constant problem for birds in cities, but can be remedied by: - Moving plants away from your windows, so birds will not go for them - Putting light marks or decals on windows, so birds will know that they are solid (make your windows look cool, too!) - Turning down lights in offices that have large windows (daylight will illuminate the ofice)
