Tactful Ways to Encourage Others to Help the Birds

Preventing birds from colliding with windows

Use Shades and Blinds, Mobile decorations and Opaque ones as well, to reduce light and reflection in windows and doors…. “You know, it might be a good idea to use a window shade for your door, to reduce the reflection and avoid birds flying into the glass, so you can help the birds, and yourself by not having to scoop up the bird and go through that trauma.”

Giving birds the nutrition they need

Avoid Feeding the Birds Bread at all costs…… “Did you know that instead of bread, which has no nutritional value, a safer and better option would be to feed the birds oats, grains, seeds, and dried halved grapes or frozen peas…This will help to lower the overgrowth of bacteria and algae in the water, due to extra fecal matter, and the birds can avoid fatal diseases and learn to depend on themselves and nature instead of humans…”
